Waiting for completion

The last step of the addition or removal of a recording job is visualized by the Action Progress dialog (depending on your settings). In this phase you will eventually see firewall warnings, because OTR Remote connects to the OTR servers. After the process is complete, you can close the window or wait until it is automatically closed (if enabled). If an error occurs, a message is shown and you should solve the problem. Solutions for common problems are available in the Troubleshooting document.
